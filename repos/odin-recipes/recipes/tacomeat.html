Taco Meat !

A good taco can be easier to make than you think !

This recipe is very easy and it needs juste three ingredients. One of those three is chiken as we're making chiken taco !. Let's go !

Ingredients :

  • Chiken breast (skinless and bonelles)
  • Salsa
  • Taco seasonning /li>

Steps :

  1. Put the chicken breasts in a slow-cooker with the salsa and some taco seasoning.
  2. Cook on low for 4 hours or hignt for 2 hours
  3. When the chicken taco meat is done, use two forks to shred the chicken breasts.
  4. Put the shredded chicken back into the yummy juices that have collected in the Crock-Pot, and voila!
  5. Put the garniture on tour taco and your ready to go !
